## Security & Risks

Investing in DeFi involves risk. We prioritize security, but you should always do your own research.

* **Self-Custody:** You are in control of your private keys. [Learn about Wallet Security](/developers/blockchain-essentials/browser/).
* **Audits:** All Vault contracts are audited. [View Audit Reports](/resources/).
* **Peg Security:** Learn how the [PowPeg secures your Bitcoin](https://rootstock.io/blog/bridging-without-blind-trust-how-powpeg-inherits-bitcoins-security-model/).
